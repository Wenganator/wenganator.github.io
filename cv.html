Research Profile

I develop probabilistic models and deep learning architectures to decode the neural basis of decision-making under uncertainty. My work bridges large-scale fMRI / EEG recordings with interpretable machine learning, with the goal of understanding how the brain computes value and adapts to novel environments. I am particularly interested in low-rank latent variable models and their applications to clinical neuroscience.
