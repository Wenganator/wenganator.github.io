Lord, Sovereign and Gracious, Eternal and Merciful

Lord, Sovereign and Gracious, Eternal and Merciful,
Thou art the Ruler of all realms, high and lifted up,
yet through thy Son thou stoopest down to the dust,
to gather the wayward, the weary, the lost.

Thou hast written thy story on every dawn,
marked thy sovereignty in the dance of stars,
and shown thy mercy in the gift of grace.

Before time began, thou didst ordain my days;
before I drew breath, thou didst set thy love upon me.
In thy wisdom, thou hast woven my steps
into the tapestry of thy will,
and even my wandering serves thy purposes.

Teach me, O Lord, that thy ways are not my ways,
that thy plans are deeper than the oceans,
that thy mercy is wider than the heavens,
that thy justice and love flow as one mighty river.

Let me know that to be humbled is to be lifted,
that to be broken is to be healed,
that to surrender is to find victory in thee.
Make me to see that thou art strong in my weakness,
that thy grace is sufficient when I am empty,
that thy Son is my righteousness,
when all I bring is rags of shame.

Through Jesus Christ, thou hast bound my wounds,
turned my mourning into joy,
and called me thine own, though I am unworthy.
For he was bruised that I might be healed,
stricken that I might be redeemed,
abandoned that I might be embraced,
pierced that I might be cleansed.

Lord, let thy sovereignty quiet my fears,
thy grace still my striving,
thy love anchor my heart.
Teach me to rest in thy mighty hands,
to trust in thy eternal goodness,
and to lean upon the cross where love and justice meet.

In the storms, be my calm;
in the trials, my refuge;
in the valley, my vision of heavenâ€™s light.
For though I am but dust and ashes,
thou hast crowned me with mercy
and clothed me in the righteousness of thy Son.

Make me thy servant, willing and obedient,
that my life might testify of thy sovereign grace,
and my heart rejoice in the fullness of Christ.

To thee, O Lord, be all glory, majesty,
dominion, and authority,
before all time, now, and forever.

Amen.
